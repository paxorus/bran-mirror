Welcome to BranPlan!
BranPlan is a free Web app, and a new way to think about your four-year plan at Brandeis.
It features a simple drag-and-drop interface for easy manipulation and multiple saveable schedules.
If you're a student who's trying to fit several majors or minors, or needs to watch the number of credits each semester,
or ensuring that all graduation or premedical requirements are fulfilled, then I hope that BranPlan suits your needs.
